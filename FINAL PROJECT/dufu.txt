QUESTION
A Scholar Tracking System
Develop a web-based Scholar Tracking System
for Dufuna task submission. This system will have 3 types of users:
ADMIN
The Admin should be able to
-Log into the app
-Pair scholars to their  mentors
-Schedule, manage and track curriculum and tasks
SCHOLAR
A scholar should be able to
-Sign up on the app
-Sign into the app
-Submit tasks
-Comment on tasks submitted
-View submission status
MENTOR
A mentor should be able to
-Sign up on the app
-Sign into the app
-Review and comment on submissions
-Update submission status (from pending
to accepted)
